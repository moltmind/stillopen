FAQ

Questions med spa owners ask me.

Will it make medical claims I can't back up?+

No. The bot is trained only on what's on your website. It quotes the prices and services you publish. If a client asks about contraindications, dosing, or anything clinical it doesn't have explicit content on, it says "let me have the nurse injector call you back tomorrow" and texts you the lead. The boundary is hard-coded.

What about HIPAA?+

The Pro tier is HIPAA-considerate by design. The bot doesn't ask for medical history, current medications, or any PHI. It only collects what a public-facing inquiry form would collect (name, phone, service interest, preferred time). Anything clinical is routed to a callback. If you need a full BAA-covered solution, talk to me before you buy.

I already use Boulevard / Mindbody / GlossGenius. Why do I need this?+

Those are great for the workflow AFTER she becomes a client. StillOpen catches her in the 30 seconds between "found you on Instagram" and "ghosted to the next spa." It sits on top of your existing booking system and writes appointments to your Google Calendar (which Boulevard/Mindbody pull from). It doesn't replace anything you already pay for.

Will it sound like a chatbot?+

It sounds like a receptionist who's worked at your spa for years. It knows your services menu, your pricing, your hours, your service area. It uses contractions. It doesn't dump features. The Pro tier lets you set a custom voice (warm clinical, luxe, conversational) so it matches your brand.

What if a client cancels after taking the deposit?+

Deposits land on YOUR Stripe, not ours. You control the refund policy the same way you'd handle any other client payment. Most med spas keep a $100-$200 deposit that applies to the service total, refundable up to 24 hours before. Your rules, your money.

How long does setup take?+

48 hours from purchase to live on your site. I scan your site, train the AI on your services and prices, send you a preview, you approve it, I paste a one-line snippet (or text it to you to paste). If I miss the 48-hour window, full refund.

What if I want to cancel?+

Cancel in one click. No phone call, no cancellation survey, no "are you sure." You keep all your bookings and your Stripe data. I don't hold your customer information hostage.
