**How does the AI know what to say about my shop?**
It reads your website. It picks up your services, your service area, your hours, your phone. If it doesn't know something, it says "let me have someone from the team call you back with the exact details" and takes the customer's name and number. It never invents a price or a policy.

**Do I have to change my website to make it work?**
No. You paste one line of code inside your body tag. Works with WordPress, Wix, Squarespace, Webflow, hand-coded sites. If you can't paste it yourself, I do it.

**What if a customer asks something weird?**
The AI is trained to stay inside your shop's known services and prices. If a customer asks something the AI doesn't know, it captures their name and callback number. You get the message. You call them when you can.
