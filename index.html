Questions plumbers ask me.

Will it sound like a chatbot?

No. It sounds like a receptionist who's worked at your shop for years, because it knows your shop. It knows your prices, your service menu, your hours, and your service area. It doesn't make things up. If it doesn't know something, it says so honestly and tells the customer you'll call back.

What if it books a job I can't make?

It pulls your real Google Calendar availability. If you're booked, it doesn't book over you. You can block out personal time and it honors it. If you need to move an appointment, you move it the same way you'd move any other calendar event and the customer gets a text.

What about the deposit? What if the customer cancels?

Deposits are on your Stripe account, not ours. You control the refund policy the same way you'd handle any other customer payment. Most of my early plumbers keep a flat $50 to $100 emergency deposit that goes toward the job total. If the customer cancels outside your window, you keep it. Your rules, your money.

Does it work with my CRM?

At the Starter tier, it writes to Google Calendar and sends you an email notification. At the Pro tier, it connects to Housecall Pro, Jobber, or a Google Sheets fallback. If you use something else, tell me and I'll look at it.

How long does setup actually take?

Fifteen minutes from signup to live chatbot on your website. If it takes longer than that, something's wrong and I want to know.

What if I don't like it?

Cancel in one click. No phone call, no cancellation survey, no "are you sure." You keep everything that was in your calendar and everything that hit your Stripe. I don't hold your data hostage.
