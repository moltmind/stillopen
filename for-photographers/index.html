FAQ

Questions photographers ask me.

Will the bot quote dates that aren't actually open?+

No. It reads your real Google Calendar in real time. If a Saturday is blocked, it doesn't offer it. If you mark a personal weekend, it honors it. The only way it books a wrong slot is if your calendar is wrong, and you'd have that problem with a human assistant too.

I use Honeybook / Dubsado / Pixieset. Why do I need this?+

Honeybook and Dubsado are workflow tools for AFTER she becomes a client. They handle contracts, invoices, galleries. They don't catch the bride at 11:38 PM before she ghosts to the next photographer. StillOpen sits on top of your existing stack and writes confirmed bookings to your Google Calendar, which Honeybook/Dubsado pull from. Nothing replaces what you already pay for.

Will it sound like a chatbot to my brides?+

It sounds like a thoughtful assistant who's worked at your studio for a year. It uses contractions. It asks one question at a time. It doesn't dump pricing in one block. The Pro tier lets you tune the voice (editorial, warm, intimate, breezy) so it matches your brand. Most brides assume they're talking to your studio manager.

What about my packages? They're complicated.+

Same way you'd describe them to a new assistant. We read your site, pull the package tiers and what's included, and review it with you before launch. If a bride asks something edge-case ("do you travel for destination weddings under 4 hours?"), the bot either quotes your real policy or says "let me have [you] follow up on that one tomorrow."

What if a bride pays the deposit and cancels?+

Deposits land on YOUR Stripe, not ours. You control the refund policy. Most photographers keep the discovery call deposit fully refundable up to 24 hours before, then non-refundable after that. Your call. Your contract. Your money.

How long does setup take?+

48 hours from purchase to live on your site. I scan your portfolio site, train the AI on your packages and pricing, send you a preview, you approve it, I paste one line of code (or text it for you to paste). If I miss 48 hours, full refund.

I'm in wedding season. I don't have time to set this up.+

That's the point. You don't set it up. I do. You answer four questions in a text thread with me, paste a line of code on your site (or I do it), and it's live. 48 hours. You go back to shooting.
