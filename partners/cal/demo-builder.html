Demo Builder · The Magic Moment

During a call, paste the plumber's website URL. The bot scrapes their real services, prices, and hours. You get a personalized demo URL in 30 seconds to text them.
How to use this (read once, then it's muscle memory)
  1. Get them on the phone first. Don't use this tool cold. Use it when you're on a call and the plumber says "sure, send me a demo."
  2. Ask for their website URL. "What's your shop's website? I'll build you a demo right now while we're talking." If they don't have one, skip the URL and just use their shop name + Lake City.
  3. Paste the URL below and hit Build. The tool scrapes their site for 15-25 seconds. Talk to them while it's working. Ask how long they've been in business. Ask what their busiest service is. Keep them engaged.
  4. Hit Copy Text when it's ready. Paste into iMessage to them. The text includes the demo URL and a callback line.
  5. Send while they're still on the call. "Just sent you the demo. Tap the link. The bot you'll see is using your real shop's services. Talk to it like a customer at 11pm."
  6. Lock the callback. "I'll call you back tomorrow same time to walk you through what you saw." Hang up. Mark them DEMO in your command center.

If the scrape can't find enough on their site (rare), the demo still works with industry defaults. Never breaks. Always sendable. Tomorrow on the callback, you ask "did you talk to it? What did you ask it?" That conversation closes the deal.

Paste their site without https:// if it's easier. The tool adds it. You can grab the URL directly from your browser address bar if you have their site open.
