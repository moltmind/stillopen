FAQ

Questions agency owners ask me.

How long does the white-label setup take?+

About 24 hours from signed partner agreement. You get the agency dashboard, branding controls, your wholesale Stripe portal, and access to the partner Slack channel. After that, each client install takes about 15 minutes.

Do my clients ever see the StillOpen name?+

Only if you want them to. Default is fully white-labeled. Your agency name on the dashboard, your branding in the customer notifications, your support email in the chat footer. From your client's perspective, this is your product.

Who handles support if a client has a problem?+

You're the first line. I back you up. There's a partner Slack channel I monitor personally. If something breaks on our end, I fix it the same day. If a real bug costs a client a booking, I refund you the wholesale that month.

What if I bring 25 clients on and one of them cancels?+

You drop them from your dashboard. The wholesale rate is per active install per month. No contract on your client's end. Same flexibility you give them on any other recurring service.

Can I mark up however I want?+

Yes. Most partners price at $197-$497/mo to the end client depending on vertical. Med spas, dental practices, law firms tolerate higher prices. Trades and small fitness studios price lower. Your call. Your customer relationship, your pricing decision.

What if I want to test it with one client first?+

Fine. We'll set you up as a partner for a single install at the per-install wholesale rate. If you bring 5+ clients in 90 days, we'll credit your account toward the Founding 10 Partner pricing.

How is this different from Avoca, Smith.ai, or Podium?+

Those are enterprise plays gated behind sales demos at $500-$1,500/mo per location. They don't white-label. They don't let you resell. StillOpen is built for the agency-resold-to-SMB layer they ignore. Cheaper per seat. Transparent pricing. You keep the customer relationship instead of handing it to a sales team.

What if your product breaks and embarrasses me with a client?+

Fair concern. I'm in the partner Slack same day. If a client install ever has a hard bug that costs them a booking, I refund you the install fee and that month's wholesale. I'm not perfect. I ship fixes the day they're reported.
