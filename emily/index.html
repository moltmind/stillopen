    A Wisconsin Photographer:
    Real, Energetic, Emotive Photography

    Named one of the best photographers in Wisconsin, Emily Kettner Photography combines editorial photography with documentary photojournalism in an easy and comfortable experience, to bring you the best of both worlds for your special moments in life.

     

    When you book a photography session with Emily, expect a combination of traditional photos with candid moments for a casual, comfortable session. Photography sessions are not done in a studio setting — instead Emily uses natural surroundings including your home and the outdoors to take photos, for light, bright, true-to-life colors and style.

     

    Emily works across Wisconsin, including Appleton, Green Bay, Milwaukee and Madison to photograph weddings, engagements and seniors, and loves to travel to destination weddings as well.

    about emily

    My husband gave me my first professional camera as our wedding gift, and ever since, I have been taking photos. I took them professionally for years, but up until 2020, I was the Senior Vice President of an Advertising Agency out of Florida as well. When Covid-19 happened, I asked myself, why was I wasting time behind a desk when what made me happiest was taking photos? I couldn't kick that feeling — even if that meant quitting a secure and well-paid job — and so I started a business in 2020. Yep, I quit my job during a pandemic to follow my passion.​
