Meet Your Dentist

James S. Kim, DDS

Dr. James S. Kim received his Bachelor of Science from Loyola University of Chicago and his DDS from the University of Illinois College of Dentistry. He has maintained a private practice in Wausau since 2000, with an emphasis in family, cosmetic, implant, and sedation dentistry.

Dr. Kim is committed to staying current in the dental field, attending 150–200 hours of continuing education per year — far beyond what is required. He has trained with some of the best clinicians in the world, including Dr. Frank Spear at The Seattle Institute for Advanced Dental Education and Dr. Carl Misch at The Misch International Implant Institute.
