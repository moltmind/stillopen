Just to see you smile.

Providing Eau Claire and the Chippewa Valley with exceptional, personalized dental care since 1973.

At Oakwood Hills Family Dental, we value our patient relationships, making it our priority to deliver gentle, compassionate care to everyone we meet. Starting from the moment you walk in, we work hard to make you feel at ease. Through our beautiful, state of the art office and the latest dental technology, our professional and compassionate staff strive to provide you with the best oral heath care possible. The result is a beautiful, healthy smile that can last a lifetime.
